Frequently Asked Questions

Advanced inverter solutions designed for efficiency, reliability, and seamless energy management.

What makes Suntec inverters different from conventional systems?

Our inverters utilize AI-driven optimization to deliver 99.7% conversion efficiency, adapting in real-time to weather conditions and energy demands while minimizing power loss.

How durable are Suntec inverters in extreme weather?

Engineered with military-grade components, our systems operate flawlessly from -30°C to 65°C and feature IP65-rated waterproof enclosures for monsoon/desert climates.

Can the inverter integrate with existing solar setups?

Yes, our universal compatibility design supports hybrid configurations with lithium/lead-acid batteries, grid-tie systems, and third-party solar panels without performance compromises.

What happens during power outages?

With less than 10ms switchover time and 48-hour battery backup capacity, critical appliances remain powered seamlessly during blackouts.

How does remote monitoring work?

The Suntec IoT Hub provides live energy analytics, fault alerts, and consumption reports via smartphone, with 24/7 cloud-based diagnostics.

What maintenance is required?

Our fanless, solid-state design ensures zero routine maintenance for 10+ years, backed by automated self-checks and corrosion-resistant materials.
